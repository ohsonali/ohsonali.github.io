Flight Line of The Future

Problem Statement

This project, by NSIN X-Force interns Bernard Chan and Sonali Loomba, is centered around streamlining aircraft maintenance for Travis Air Force Base. Presently, the first steps of the maintenance process begin with identifying the malfunctioning part on the flight line, and subsequently putting in an order for that part. The current method of ordering a part uses a multitude of platforms and requires inquiry requests to different departments before a formal supply order can be submitted. This process can be unwieldy and there is room for improvement to make it more efficient.
